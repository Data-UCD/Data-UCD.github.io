Davis Data Science Club

The first and only student-run club focused on the multidisciplinary field of Data Science.

About Us

The Davis Data Science Club, recently merged with the UC Davis Statistics Club, is the premier student run Data Science organization at UC Davis. Home to one of the top Statistics departments in the world. We conduct in-depth workshops, host events conducted by experienced professionals at the biggest technology companies in Northern California, and offer hands-on experience outside the classroom through our project groups.

Meetings and Events

We hold meetings every Thursday at 5:30 pm in MSB 1147

We host professional speakers, workshops, and events from leading technology companies.

Contact Us

If you're interested in potential collaboration or would like more information feel free to email us or reach us through Facebook and LinkedIn.
